About The Monal

Our History

The Monal is one of Pakistan’s most iconic restaurant destinations, located in the scenic hills of Islamabad. Over the years, it has become a symbol of luxury dining combined with breathtaking natural views.

Our Story

At The Monal, we take pride in offering authentic Pakistani cuisine that reflects a rich cultural heritage. Our flavors are inspired by influences from Arabia, Persia, and Central Asia, blending centuries of tradition into every dish.

Our chefs use traditional cooking techniques with a modern touch, ensuring every meal delivers both quality and unforgettable taste.

Our Experience

Beyond food, The Monal offers an experience — a perfect combination of ambience, service, and panoramic views. Whether it's a family dinner or a special occasion, we aim to create lasting memories.
